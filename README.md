# Omega ISO Case

Meme-Board praising the ISO Enter

[PCB by Piit](https://github.com/piit79/omega-iso-keyboard)

![OmegaISO](https://github.com/Technofrikus/Omega-Iso-Case/blob/master/images/caserender.png)

More pictures and infos at [Moinboards](https://moinboards.de/projects/isoding/).

BOM for the case
- fixing top and bottom together:
	- 10x M3 (L4mm) Heatset inserts, Voron Style, for fixing top and bottom together
	- 10x M3x10 countersunk screws (M3x8 socket head works as well but doesnt look as nice)
- mounting the plate to the case and fixing the weight
	- 18x M2 (L3mm) Heatset inserts
	- 18x M2x4-5mm socket head screws


Assembly
- Badge on top housing: just glew it in
- Assemble left and rights (doveteil) if applicable
- Put heat inserts in
- Mount Plate (assembled with PCB)
- Put in the weight according to this picture below
- assemble top and bottom

![Weight](https://github.com/Technofrikus/Omega-Iso-Case/blob/master/images/inside.png)
